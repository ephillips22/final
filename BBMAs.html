The BBMAs



Last night (May 20th) was a big night for music and for BTS. They won the Top Social Artist Award at an American award show for the second year in a row!
The boys also performed their new song "Fake Love" to a roaring crowd of ARMY (BTS fans) for the first time! They met DJ Khalid, Taylor Swift, Pharell Williams, and many other stars they idolize (while wearing all Gucci of course)!






Hello!/ Anneyohaseyo/ 안녕하세요

  1. I love you: saranghae: 사랑해
  2. BTS: Bangtan Sondeyonaen: 방탄소년단
  3. Yes: Nae: 예
  4. No: Ani: 아니
  5. American: Amelikaui: 아메리카의
  6. Please: Jebal: 제발
  7. Thank you: Kamsamnida: 감사합니다


Note: Just like English, there are many ways to say the same thing. These are not the formal, official terms to use, but they are the ones I have learned through listening to a lot of KPOP!
