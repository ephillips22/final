More on the Band

BTS has many songs in English and Korean, but music is a universal language. Just because I can't understand what they're saying, doesn't mean I can't feel what they're saying. Subtitles are provided on most videos and once you listen to enough songs, you can pick out key words to understand what is going on. However, for me, the most important thing is the sound. The way their voices blend and the arrangement of their songs is absolutely beautiful to me.

Recently, BTS landed in L.A for their performance at the BBMAs (Billboard Music Awards) on May 20th!!
